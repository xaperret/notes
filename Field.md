# Field

## Field in few words

## Field in details

A [[Field]] is something that is declared inside a [[Class]] outside a [[Method]] or [[Constructor]] or [[Initializer]].
A [[Field]] represents the **structure** of an [[Object]] and its _value_ is the **state** of the [[Object]].
